A Global Basketball Journey Shaped How I Solve Problems

From NYC and proud of my Belizean heritage, I’ve lived and competed in Spain, El Salvador, Mexico, and Belize. Playing Professional Basketball Overseas has taught me discipline, composure, adaptability, and how to perform under pressure.

Today, I bring that same mindset & work ethnic into my tech career in analytics and consulting. At Visa Consulting & Analytics, I help retail merchant clients optimize digital payment ecosystems and translate complex data into strategic recommendations.

My toolkit includes SQL, Python, Tableau, Power BI, Excel, Snowflake, Databricks, AWS/GCP, Sales, Business Development, Storytelling, and a Deep Curiosity about AI, Fintech, Payments, and Sports Analytics.
